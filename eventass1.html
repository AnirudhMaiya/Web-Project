  • Gaming or comparable industry experience in growing organically through known and new channels
  • Outstanding written, oral, and organizational ability
  • Must have the passion to self-motivate, the guile to sell your ideas to other stakeholders, and the drive to execute those ideas cross-functionally
  • Love for games and creating fun, compelling and addictive user experiences
  • Ability to operate in an entrepreneurial environment, to thrive at an extremely fast pace and to find creative ways to get things done
