The UI/UX Design Intern will work closely with Producers, Product Managers, and Engineers to help design, mock and implement feature UI and UX flows.

1)Responsibilities:

  • Work closely with Producers and PM's to create simple creative solutions to complicated design needs
  • Mock up and prototype features according to spec
  • Provide variety of solutions to design needs
  • Work closely with engineers to implement features UI and UX flows
  • Deliver exciting, effective, memorable, polished UI/UX
  • Implement motion and interaction design
  • Clearly communicate experienced design rationale; have impeccable judgment
  • Rapidly iterate and test a variety of ideas using wireframes, sketches and fully designed comps when needed
  • Partner with engineers to collaborate and iterate in fast-paced design-to-build cycles and ensure your designs are implemented to specification
  • Stay ahead of the curve in technological advances and UI practices
  • Make a point to understand how youplay's audience interacts with its games/services, and make use of this knowledge in the development of the best possible user experiences
  • Ability to rapidly switch pace depending on projects needs

2)How to Apply:

  • Submit your resume including a link to your online portfolio/website.

  • You, get "Them Certificates"
  • Delicious food! Yes, our interns partake in our delicious (and freeeeeeeeeeeeeeeeee!) gourmet meals every day - all breakfast and lunches are provided Monday through Friday! And delicious snacks all day every day!
  • We also provide free gym memberships at certain locations.
